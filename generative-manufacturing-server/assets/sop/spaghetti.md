# Spaghetti Failure SOP

## Diagnosis
Filament is not adhering to the print or bed, creating a tangled mess resembling spaghetti.

## Immediate Actions
1. **STOP** the printer immediately.
2. Clear the bed of all failed material.

## Prevention
* Check bed adhesion (clean bed, check temperature).
* Verify Z-offset calibration.
* Check filament quality (moisture).
