# Bed Adhesion SOP

## Diagnosis
First layer does not stick to the bed, dragging around or warping excessively.

## Immediate Actions
1. **STOP** the print.
2. Clean the print bed with Isopropyl Alcohol (IPA).

## Adjustments
* Recalibrate First Layer Z-height (Live Adjust Z).
* Increase Bed Temperature by 5-10Â°C.
* Use a brim for objects with small contact area.
